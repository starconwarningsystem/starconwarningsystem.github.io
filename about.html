About The STARCON Warning System

The Star vs the Forces of Evil Community Network (STARCON) Warning System is a nonprofit group that measures the current status of the Star vs. the Forces of Evil community and episode/season status. The STARCON Warning System is not affiliated with any Disney XD agency or other branch of Disney or their developers. The goal of this site is to give warnings of the level of tension or danger among the community including but not limited to: high activity, death threats, cyber bullying, etc. After the Steven Universe Fandom incident with death threats towards Fan Art artists for drawing characters as skinnier than they are, the Founder of this site has decided to make a warning system for SVTFOE to keep an eye on the status in hopes of never reaching that level of danger (Level/STARCON 1). This system is based off of the U.S. Military's Defence Military Readiness Condition (DEFCON) Warning System and has found to be effective and easy to use or understand.

*The STARCON Warning System is not in any way affiliated with Starco or any SVTFOE ships.

Thus far we have been in STARCON 5 two times, STARCON 4 six times, STARCON 3 five times, and STARCON 2 and 1 none.

About the Founder

Hello everybody! My name is Benjamin; some on Steam know me as Exotic Butters, Awesome1pc2, DEFCON Warning System, or Anti Sea Bear Circle.

I was brought to the show by Zach (below) and have seen every episode since. I decided to check out the Fandom and iFunny and found craziness one day. Since then I decided to gather data on this and create a website on it to save other's time. I had previously known of the DEFCON Warning System and decided to base this site off of it. It turned out to be more difficult than expected as there is a lot less information on this topic. I found the major areas of interest of the SVTFOE community and decided to focus on those for the STARCON information you see when you visit the site. I didn't really start doing anything until "Bon Bon the Birthday Clown", which marked my first update to the website. Since then I have been devoted to following a timed schedule found in the "Our Color System" tab to keep everybody up-to-date with the current status of the community. Also, with the SVTFOE Marathon, "Just Friends", and the Season 2 Finale coming up, I even decided to in these moments add a countdown timer and create in-depth analysis of certain aspects of the most important episodes as seen in the 2/21/17 update.

Just thought I'd throw this out there, I am the only full-time developer of the site, so interface updates may be delayed due to resources, skill, and/or school. Also, over time the updates will become more detailed and factual than they already are.

Enjoy, and best wishes,
Benjamin, Founder and Head of The STARCON Warning System
