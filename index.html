​​​The STARCON Warning System _ Update 11/12/17

This is The STARCON Warning System. Alert status for 8 P.M. EST, Sunday, November 12, 2017. Condition code is Blue. STARCON 4.

There are currently no threats against the Star vs. the Forces of Evil community and developers at this time. However, further monitoring is required.

With the SVTFOE marathon going on right now, the community has had to rapidly adjust to new information being thrown at them, causing some instability. This update contains spoilers.

After Marco left Mewni in the first episode of the marathon, many people thought that we may not see Marco again for some time. From there, the Startom ship became canon, which caused many Starco shippers to jump ship and lose hope in the show. Jarco ended up breaking up to the surprise of many viewers, and Marco returned to Mewni. The community at first did not know how to react to this, but the overall consensus was that they were glad Marco was not out of the picture this season.

The first episode with Marco back at Mewni caught many people off guard. For one, Star seemed to be, ironically, against Marco's return to Mewni. The community did eventually determine that Star was just surprised and did not expect Marco to return to Mewni. Additionally, Star and Tom were hanging out, so Marco's return interrupted what they were doing. Eventually, Star accepted Marco back into her life, and the community returned to normal.

Despite most of the community returning to normal, excluding the Jarco shippers with their dead ship and Starco shippers not knowing how to respond to Startom, there are a few who got salty at the recent events. On the Starco page specifically, a few users have been responding to Startom as completely killing Starco. Others are completely leaving the show over a ship, an illogical action. Such users and actions require a closer eye on the community, so we are staying at STARCON 4 until the issues are resolved.

The STARCON Warning System is a nonprofit group that measures the current status of the Star vs. the Forces of Evil community and episode/season status. The STARCON Warning System is not affiliated with any Disney XD agency or other branch of Disney or their developers. Had this been an actual threat, The STARCON Warning System would post current threat rankings and progressing issues on their website. The next scheduled update is 8 P.M. EST on Sunday, November 20, 2017. We are now on the marathon schedule.

This concludes this broadcast of The STARCON Warning System.
