​​​The STARCON Warning System _ Update 8/1/17

This is The STARCON Warning System. Alert status for 8 P.M. EDT, Tuesday, August 1, 2017. Condition code is Yellow. STARCON 3.

There are currently growing threats against the Star vs. the Forces of Evil community and developers at this time.

After Disney XD Tweeted a scene from Season 3 showing Star and Tom dancing together, many Starco shippers have begun hating Daron Nefcy and abandoning ship. The threat produced by the Starco community is steadily growing as they hate Daron and her team. The situation is still too new to predict its direction. Until the threat settles down, we are on STARCON 3. If the threat continues to grow, we may increase the level to 2

The iFunny is still normal, but the Fandom is far from, especially on the Starco page. People are typing in all caps to express anger towards Daron's not making Starco canon and treating Starco shippers "poorly" with "heart-wrecking" moments. The STARCON Warning System has attempted to calm the community down in the time being, reminding them that the clip is not valid evidence against the ship and technically the ship itself in the actual show is fairly new (having not technically existed prior to Star's admitting her crush on Marco). It seems despite these actions the shippers are arguing back anyways and still hating Daron. The STARCON Warning System will continue to monitor the situation and keep it under control.

The STARCON Warning System is a nonprofit group that measures the current status of the Star vs. the Forces of Evil community and episode/season status. The STARCON Warning System is not affiliated with any Disney XD agency or other branch of Disney or their developers. Had this been an actual threat, The STARCON Warning System would post current threat rankings and progressing issues on their website. The next scheduled update is 8 P.M. EDT on Tuesday, August 22, 2017. We are on the hiatus schedule.

This concludes this broadcast of The STARCON Warning System.
