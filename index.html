This is based off of The DEFCON Warning System. For more information, click here.

​​​The STARCON Warning System _ Update 6/13/17

This is The STARCON Warning System. Alert status for 8 P.M. EDT, Tuesday, June 13, 2017. Condition code is Green. STARCON 5.

There are currently no major imminent threats against the Star vs. the Forces of Evil community or developers at this time.

Disney XD released today the SVTFOE Season 3 Intro on Instagram, saying that they couldn't keep it secret, referencing the leak of the intro from the previous month. Naturally, the iFunny tag exploded, but instead of just crazy excitement like with promos, new SVTFOE memes are taking over.

Disney XD had announced a few weeks ago that Season 3 would be released on July 15, 2017 in the form of a 2-hour-long episode titled "The Battle for Mewni". This one enormous episode is supposed to take the place of the first four episodes of the season, so the rest of the season is not expected to pick up until mid-August.

The STARCON Warning System is a nonprofit group that measures the current status of the Star vs. the Forces of Evil community and episode/season status. The STARCON Warning System is not affiliated with any Disney XD agency or other branch of Disney or their developers. Had this been an actual threat, The STARCON Warning System would post current threat rankings and progressing issues on their website. The next scheduled update is 8 P.M. EDT on Friday, July 16, 2017.

This concludes this broadcast of The STARCON Warning System.
