Our Color System

We use an updated form of The DEFCON Warning System's original color system; our colors are the exact same as The DEFCON Warning System's modern colors. Also listed are update frequencies, what each STARCON level stands for, and they do corroborate with The DEFCON Warning System's levels, just applied to The STARCON Warning System's purposes.

STARCON 1 - Red - Major threats towards developers or other fans - 12 hours
STARCON 2 - Orange - Lots of arguing or hate, minor threats - daily
STARCON 3 - Yellow - Endless posts of the new topic/arguing, possible threats - weekly​
STARCON 4 - Blue - Higher Craziness - every other week
STARCON 5 - Green - Normal Community - monthly

* When off season, each level's frequency of update is changed to be that of one level down (1 = daily, etc.) with STARCON 5 being no updates until either the next season's announced release, teaser trailer, or two months have passed.
