Why This Site Exists

There has been a question: "Why?". I can only assume thus far that the question is "Why does this site exist, and why do you spend so much time studying the episode to create in-depth summaries like you do?".

The answer to that was all back in the not much older days of Steven Universe. A fan artist drew one of the more obese characters as skinnier, more healthy weight. When some people found out, they gave the fan artist death threats to the point where the fan artist attempted suicide.

After hearing this story and joining the Star vs. the Forces of Evil Fandom myself (I was brought to the show by my friend, Zach, the Admin), I decided to create an alert system inspired by The DEFCON Warning System to alert the Fandom of any possible threats with STARCON 1 being death threats and attempted suicide by a member or creator and STARCON 5 being a normal Fandom. We have been monitoring every episode and Fandom reaction on iFunny and the Fandom itself ever since to ensure that all viewers are aware of any issues or threats in the community.

We listen to fellow SVTFOE fans to get their perspective and any ideas out or get info on threats we never knew about or saw as a threat. Since we currently are not getting any input, everything is just us posting our ideas, thoughts, and perspectives on potential threats from episodes and actual threats in the community.

Most of this can be found in the About Us tab of the site under "About The STARCON Warning System".

-Benjamin, Founder and Head of The STARCON Warning System

It's Star's Fault?

There have been many people complaining on iFunny and the Fandom about Marco's giving more attention to Jackie than to Star after Star nearly got sucked into a "Mystic Room Suck Transform". What most people don't know was it wasn't entirely Marco's fault. Rather, Star held most of the blame.

For one, Star used the "All-Seeing Eye" and tried to hurt Marco and Jackie when they were riding the skateboard. Later, Star tried to suck up Jackie with the "Mystic Room Suck Transform". This isn't much evidence towards my point, but it shows how Star caused all of the issues in her jealousy. The real reasons are as follows. People are more likely to show more emotion towards their date than towards another friend. It's just a fact. Additionally, you'll show even more emotion towards your date after your first kiss with them, which is exactly what happened in the episode. Also, Star had a whole month to do something about Marco, like tell him that she has a crush on him or discourage him from talking with Jackie, but instead she did nothing but help and encourage him towards Jackie. She put all of her problems upon herself; Marco is not to blame. Marco even set aside the excitement of his first kiss to go find Star and save her from the "Mystic Room Suck Transform" when the Blood Moon mysteriously appeared. He sacrificed himself with Star to go with her into the portal when he could've stayed behind with Jackie. Fortunately, the others also helped save Star from the portal, so the two were never sucked in.

Therefore, even if you disagree with what happened in the episode, don't say anything. Everything that happened in that episode would have happened that same way to you if you were in that situation. Let the storyline progress on its own path; you may find that things may get better than they would have been had none of these events happened.
